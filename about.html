Welcome to PhishDetect, an innovative solution dedicated to protecting users from the growing threat of phishing websites. At the core of PhishDetect is a sophisticated machine learning model based on Random Forest, a powerful ensemble learning technique renowned for its high accuracy and robustness. Our system meticulously analyzes website content and structural features to discern legitimate sites from malicious ones. By leveraging a comprehensive dataset of known phishing and legitimate URLs, PhishDetect is trained to identify subtle patterns and anomalies that are indicative of phishing attempts. Our goal is to provide users with a reliable tool that enhances their online security by alerting them to potential threats before any harm can occur. PhishDetect is not just a technological solution; it is a commitment to safeguarding your digital experience, ensuring that you can browse the web with confidence and peace of mind.
